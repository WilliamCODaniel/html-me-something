Captains

Shark

Shark is THE goal threat captain in all of Guild Ball. Whether kicking off or receiving, Shark has the tools to score from almost anywhere on the pitch. He wants the ball and the glory. With the right combination of Squaddies, a Shark led team is most likely to get a 3 goal win.

Corsair

Corsair is the beater of the Fisherman's Guild. A Corsair led squad will be a little more focused on takeouts than goals, while still being flexible enough to get a score or two. Corsair wants to control his opponents movements via a variety of abilities that draw them in. Add Kraken to a Corsair team and you've got a strong one-two punch.

Yukai

Yukai is the new Captain on the wharf, having only been availible since March, 2019. Yukai is more akin to Shark than Corsair yet feels incredibly unique when on the pitch. Yukai wants to coach their squad to victory by passing out buffs and, in the case of their Legendary Play, extreme repositioning. A Yukai led team will mainly focus on goals and will rarely try to do much else. Bring Fathom and Angel along for a potential 2 goal activation!
